🎬 Netflix Content Analytics Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This Netflix Content Analytics Dashboard provides an interactive analysis of Netflix Movies and TV Shows using Tableau. The dashboard helps users explore content distribution, ratings, genres, release trends, and geographical availability across countries.

The goal of this project is to uncover insights about Netflix's content library and identify trends in content production, ratings, and genre popularity.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze the distribution of Movies and TV Shows on Netflix.

Explore content availability across different countries.

Identify the most common content ratings.

Discover the most popular genres.

Analyze content growth trends over the years.

Provide detailed information for individual titles.

📈 Key Insights

Content Mix

Movies represent approximately 68% of Netflix's library.

TV Shows account for approximately 32%.

Most Common Ratings

TV-MA is the most prevalent rating.

TV-14 is the second most common rating.

Popular Genres

Documentaries lead among all genres.

International content and Drama categories have strong representation.

Growth Trend

Rapid expansion in Netflix content additions between 2016 and 2019.

Significant increase in both Movies and TV Shows during this period.

Global Reach

Netflix content is distributed across numerous countries worldwide.

The United States contributes the largest volume of content.
